### Problem
Humanitarian teams frequently need to build dashboards quickly, but repeated setup work delays operational use.

### Work
I worked on dashboard and mapping components that standardized how HXL-tagged data is transformed and displayed for fast deployment.

### Delivery
The project established a practical baseline for repeatable, low-friction dashboard creation in response contexts.
