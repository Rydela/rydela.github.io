### Problem
Assessment outputs were scattered across organizations, making it hard to see who had assessed what and where.

### Work
I supported development of an interactive visualization to consolidate survey metadata and make cross-actor review easier.

### Delivery
The dashboard gave analysts a clearer shared view of assessment activity during its active period.
